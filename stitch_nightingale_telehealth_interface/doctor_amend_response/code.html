Management Plan: Migraine Management

Dear Sarah,

Thank you for sharing the detailed history of your migraines. Based on the frequency and severity you described (occurring 3-4 times per month), I recommend a dual approach focusing on both acute treatment and identifying triggers.

Prescription & Dosage

Sumatriptan 50mg tablets. Take ONE tablet at the first sign of a migraine. May repeat once after 2 hours if required. Do not exceed 100mg in 24 hours.

It is crucial to maintain a headache diary for the next month. Please record:

  • Date and time of onset
  • Potential triggers (dietary, stress, sleep patterns)
  • Effectiveness of the medication

If you notice any "red flag" symptoms such as sudden onset "thunderclap" headache, fever, or neck stiffness, please seek immediate emergency care.

I have scheduled a follow-up telehealth appointment in 4 weeks to review your diary and adjust the plan if necessary.

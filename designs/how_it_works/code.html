The Digital Bedside Manner.

Australia's premier asynchronous healthcare platform. Connect with AHPRA-registered doctors for prescriptions, referrals, and medical advice without the waiting room.

1

Initiate Consult

Select your medical concern and complete a brief questionnaire. Your clinical history is captured securely for doctor review.

2

Doctor Assessment

An Australian-qualified doctor reviews your submission within hours. They assess suitability based on clinical guidelines.

3

Treatment Plan

If approved, you'll receive a digital prescription (eScript) or referral via SMS and email. Our doctors may request follow-up chat details.

4

Medication Delivery

Take your eScript to any pharmacy, or opt for discreet express delivery straight to your door from our partner pharmacies.

5

Ongoing Care

Need a follow-up? Chat with your doctor at any time through our secure patient portal. Your health records are centralized for easy access.

When Not To Use Nightingale

Our platform is designed for non-emergency healthcare only. For your safety, we cannot provide care for the following:

close Medical Emergencies (Chest pain, Difficulty breathing)
close Schedule 8 (Controlled) Drug Prescriptions
close Complex Chronic Condition Management
close Conditions requiring physical examination
close Active injuries requiring urgent sutures or casting
close Mental health crises or acute suicidal ideation

Emergency Protocol

In case of emergency, immediately call 000 or visit your nearest Emergency Department.
